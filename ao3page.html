My dearest Lavinia,

It is past midnight, and I am writing by the small lamp beside the bed because sleep keeps refusing me whenever my heart is too full of you.

I miss you even when you have only just left the room. That feels impossible, and yet it is the truest thing I know. Loving you has made every ordinary hour feel touched with gold.

There are a hundred little things about you I carry with me through the day: the way your voice softens when you say my name, the way your hands always find mine, the patient kindness in your eyes when I lose my nerve.

You have become my first thought in the morning and the quiet prayer I make at night. If love is a home, then somehow you have become mine.

When you look at me, all the walls I spent years building inside myself begin to fall away.

You make me want to be gentler, braver, more honest with the tender parts of my heart. You make me believe that devotion can be a joyful thing.

I do not know what I ever did to deserve a love as warm and steadfast as yours, but I know I want to spend my life answering it with my whole soul.

Always yours,
Roseline

My beloved Roseline,

I am writing this with the window open and your last kiss still lingering in my thoughts, and I keep smiling to myself like someone who has been trusted with a beautiful secret.

That secret is simple: I love you. I love you in the calm way the moon loves the tide and in the foolish way songs love to repeat a single name.

Before you, I treated my heart like something to be managed carefully, something to keep tidy and guarded. Then you arrived, and suddenly I wanted to leave every door unlocked for you.

I have loved you from the moment I realized how safe I felt in your presence, how even your silence could make the world seem less lonely.

You love me so gently that I am still learning not to flinch from being cherished. Every day with you teaches me a new way tenderness can live.

And every day I find myself wanting to give that tenderness back to you a hundred times over, in every word, every touch, every steady promise I know how to keep.

When you look at me, I do not feel questioned. I feel chosen.

That is a rare and holy feeling, and I hold it close.

Here is what I know with all my heart:

I want your mornings, your laughter, your worries, your hand reaching for mine under a table, your head on my shoulder at the end of a long day, your life braided gently through mine.

I want to learn the shape of your happiness so well that I can recognize it even in the dark.

I want to be the person you can come home to without fear, without performance, without having to be anything but yourself.

I am here because I love you with clear eyes, with a steady heart, and with the kind of certainty that only grows stronger the longer I stay beside you.

Not for a season. For always.

With all my love,
Lavinia

P.S. I will keep falling for you in all the small ways too: across breakfast tables, in doorways, in silence, in every life we build together.
